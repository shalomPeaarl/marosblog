BBNaija All Stars: Ilebaye qualifies for finale after emerging HoH, picks all-girl BFFs

Big Brother Naija All Stars housemate, Ilebaye, has emerged as the Head of House (HoH) for this week.
Ilebaye beat Angel to win the endurance game on Monday during the arena games.
As Head of House for this week, she has qualified for the finale of the reality show.
Ilebaye also got to select four BFFs (friends for the week) and chose Angel, Venita, Mercy and Cee-C.
The ‘Gen Z baddie’ joins Cross as a finalist in the competition.
The finale takes place next Sunday, with N120million cash up for grabs.
