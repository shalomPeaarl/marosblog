'The Black Book' is number 1 on Netflix in 12 countries

The Black Book has become a global hit, topping streaming charts in 12 countries in just three days since its debut

In a data report by Flix Patrol Premium on Monday, September 25, 2023,
the crime thriller leads the viewing
rates as one of Netflix's top 10 movies this week. It isn't just one of Netflix's top 10,
but number one in 12 countries and they include the Bahamas,
Dominican Republic, Guadeloupe, Jamaica, Kenya,
Martinique, Panama, Poland, Romania, Venezuela and our very own Nigeria.
